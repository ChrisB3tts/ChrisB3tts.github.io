About Me

Hey there, I’m Christine Betts, a nature and wildlife photographer based in North Carolina, always chasing light, quiet moments, and the wild things in between. I spend a lot of time exploring the Blue Ridge Mountains, walking the shores of the Outer Banks, and watching reflections shift on peaceful lakes. Whether it's a horse moving through the dunes on the coast or a heron lifting off from still water, I love capturing those unscripted moments in the natural world.

For me, photography is about more than just beautiful views — it’s about the feeling of being out there. The hush of the woods, the rhythm of waves, the stillness before a bird takes flight. North Carolina is full of these little wonders, and I try to reflect that through every image I make.
