Website Evaluations

Blue Sky

Blue Sky takes the tried and true form of scrollable social media and simple and no cluttered. Created by the ex CEO of Twitter, Blue Sky is a simple and easy to use social media platform.

  • Contrast: The Blue of the logo acts as the highlight color for the website, and it constasts well with the background.
  • Repetition: There is the repetition of the posts.
  • Alignment: The website is simplly aligned down the center of the screen.
  • Proximity: Everything is spaced well keeping everything compact, but not cluttered

Arngren

Arngren is a website that uhh... well it's a website. It's a Norwegian online shop for for almost everything, and it is like trying to find your way through a labyinth.

  • Contrast: To many colors, images do not match each other.
  • Repetition: There is and isn't repetition, If you look closly some parts are sectioned off and have pattern, but they are in the middle of everything else.
  • Alignment: There is no alignment in most parts of the website.
  • Proximity: Everything is together and is very cluttered. The right side of the screen is blank and could have the items spread out.
